Overview

A strong and complete user-friendly ERP solution designed specifically for college administration is University Educational ERP. Details such as student entrance, enrollment information, faculty records, class management, and subject administration are simple to administer.

Screenshots

Student Application

It handles registration and admission process for new students.

Student Records

Maintains a strong database on student details like their gender, sex, age, blood group etc. It gives an overview of the student and his personal attributes.

Faculties

Efficiently handles all information on the professors, such as their credentials, experience, and educational history. Each faculty member has a connection to an employee, allowing for proper tracking of their pay stubs and other financial perks.

Batches

By taking into account every aspect of the students and assigned faculties, the batch can be managed effectively. It provides information on the total number of students in a batch and their assigned faculties.

Student Attendance

It manages student attendance in batches.

Timetable Management

Manage schedule of taking subjects for every batch

Exam Management

Organise the many exam kinds that the university offers and evaluate each subject individually.

Exam Result

Results for each student's exam.

Fees Management

Managing student fees in accordance with their courses and amenities

Semester Management

Organise the entire semester across each department

Student Portal and Online Admission

Manage new portal design for students and Also take online admission
