Stock Force Date

Cybrosys Technologies

Force a date on stock picking

Help you to give a date on Stock picking, So that you can force Odoo to use the same date on all its related records like, stock move, quants and stock Journal entry.

By Default in Odoo,

We cannot record a stock transfer happened in a past date with its correct effect.

Even after we change the scheduled date to a past date wont do the job.

Stock move is recorded with the current date

Stock history is also recorded with the current date

Using this module,

User can give a specific date as 'Force Date' in Stock Picking

That 'Force Date' will be the date of related stock moves

Stock history is also recorded with that 'Force Date'
