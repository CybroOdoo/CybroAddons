Product Brand in Sales

Manage the Product Brands Easily.

Key Highlights

Available in Community and Enterprise.

User can add the brand inside the product form.

Group products based on product Brand.

To create brand for product go to Products Brand menu under the Product menu of Sales module

Create product Brand and add products.

It is also possible to add a brand for products directly from the form view of the product.

From the view of the products, we have the option to group them by Product Brand.

Go to the Sales menu, located under the Report menu in the sales module. Once there, switch to its pivot view to conveniently view the product brand pivot view.

Additionally, within the pivot view, we have the option to group by the Brand.

  • User can create new product brands and add products to the brand.
  • Group products based on product Brand.
  • User can Filter by product brand in pivot view.
