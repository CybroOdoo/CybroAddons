Overview

This app useful to provide different PoS Reports to do analysis. It shows the PoS sales analysis of a company in many aspects such as by order,order details, payment,salesman and so on. It can be filtered out based on different date ranges too.

Features

Community & Enterprise Support

Available in Odoo 14.0 Community and Enterprise.

Generates Report for a specific date range.

Filtering based on different aspects

Print Reports in both PDF and XLSX format.

Drilled on approach
